And if you feel like you’ve been down the same road towards them, you will understand particular things ignoring time and space limitation that you don’t before.

e.g

The concept MAKE US GREAT AGAIN,on the contrary, is limited by space: the particular space: US, while not by time.

Time that you think about some absolute works, list them out, and make some effort to bring them true.
